# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

midis2jam2 is a project built on a shared love of music and open-source collaboration. We are committed to maintaining a space where contributors and community members of all backgrounds can participate with confidence — knowing they will be treated with respect and good faith.

We expect everyone who interacts within this project's spaces to uphold these standards. This Code of Conduct applies to all GitHub activity associated with midis2jam2, including issues, pull requests, discussions, code review comments, and commit messages.

## Standards of Behavior

The following behaviors reflect the kind of community we are working to build:

- Communicating clearly and constructively, especially when disagreeing
- Offering and receiving feedback with professionalism and patience
- Acknowledging mistakes and working to correct them
- Being considerate of contributors at all experience levels
- Keeping discussions focused on the work, not the person

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or conduct targeting any individual or group
- Deliberate misrepresentation of another person's words or intentions
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider unwelcome or harmful in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you observe a violation of this Code of Conduct, please open a GitHub issue and label it clearly. Reports will be reviewed by the project maintainers.

When filing a report, include as much relevant context as possible: links to the relevant content, a description of the behavior, and any prior exchanges that provide useful background. Reporters acting in good faith will not face retaliation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and upholding these standards. Responses to violations will be proportionate to the nature and severity of the behavior:

1. **Warning** — For minor or first-time violations, a maintainer will address the behavior directly with a written warning and an explanation of why the conduct was inappropriate.

2. **Temporary ban** — For repeated violations or conduct that is clearly harmful, the individual may be temporarily restricted from participating in project spaces.

3. **Permanent ban** — For severe violations — including sustained harassment, targeted abuse, or behavior that fundamentally undermines the integrity of the community — the individual may be permanently removed from all project spaces.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face consequences at the discretion of the project's executive developer.
